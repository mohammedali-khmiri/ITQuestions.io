What is the CS50 course and what do you gain from it

The CS50 course is an introductory course that explains all the basics of computer science that you need to take the initiative to learn more specialized skills later, such as programming and development.
CS50 (or CS50X) is a course offered by the famous Harvard University.

The course helps you:
• A complete and comprehensive understanding of everything related to the basics of programming and development and its relationship to computers.
• In the self-directed course, you will acquire the techniques of programmatic and algorithmic thinking.
• Learn different programming languages, the most important of which is the C programming language, which is the mother tongue of all other languages.
• And finally, how to turn an idea into an integrated software project through structuring, analyzing, developing and finally reviewing it.
